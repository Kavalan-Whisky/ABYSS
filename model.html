On the choice to name it Polaris.

The North Star, Polaris, has been used for navigation for more than two thousand years. It does not move. Everything else does. We named this watch after it because, in 2026, we wanted to make something that does not move either — not in price, not in production volume, not in design ambition.

The case shape is identical to our 1991 first watch. The movement architecture is unchanged since 2008. The dial is a slight evolution of N° 03 from 2014. None of this is laziness. All of it is on purpose.

To acquire a Polaris, please write to acquire [at] meridian-lelocle.ch. We respond within the same week, always personally, never with a form.
