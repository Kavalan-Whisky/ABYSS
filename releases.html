HALF-LIGHT OPERATOR

LP · 9 tracks · 47:02

The colder record. Made over six months of insomnia, mostly in headphones, mostly at the wrong hour. Reviewers called it "ambient-adjacent" which is more or less true.

Includes the live favorite Trans-Atlantic Drift and a 12-minute closing piece that we never play the same way twice.
